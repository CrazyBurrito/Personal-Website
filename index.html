About

I am a Software Engineer living in Boulder, CO. Currently, I am employed at Vertafore as a Software Engineer. I graduated from The University of Colorado at Boulder with a BS degree in Computer Science. I also completed a rigorous Web Development bootcamp through Spark Boulder. Both of these experiences taught me how to develop innovative, user­friendly software solutions. While I was at CU Boulder, I was Secretary of the Science Fiction and Fantasy Club. I also worked on many computer science projects with groups of my peers. Through these experiences, I learned how to coordinate with a group and communicate effectively with the team. I've found that working well with a group of people better enables everyone to produce fantastic software. I strive to apply my computer science skills in my everyday life; I have written multiple programs in Python that analyze large data sets from websites, as well as a reddit bot that reacts to users posting on forums, providing the users with a better platform for discussions.
